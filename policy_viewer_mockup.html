Anti-Sexual Harassment Policy
Smart Civic Solutions
📅 Effective: April 2026 🔄 Version 1.0 🌐 English / Arabic 📋 Generated Mar 28, 2026
Section 1
Purpose and Commitment
Smart Civic Solutions is committed to providing a work environment free from all forms of sexual harassment. This Policy reflects our organizational values of dignity, respect, and gender equality — principles that are central to our mission as a gender equality-focused civil society organization.
This Policy applies to all staff, volunteers, consultants, partners, and any individuals engaged with Smart Civic Solutions in any capacity, both within organizational premises and in external settings including field work, events, and online interactions.
Section 2
Definitions
Sexual Harassment
Any unwelcome sexual advance, request for sexual favour, or other verbal or physical conduct of a sexual nature that affects an individual's employment, unreasonably interferes with their work performance, or creates a hostile work environment.
Gender-Based Violence (GBV)
Violence directed against a person on the basis of their gender, including sexual, physical, psychological, and economic harm.
Complainant
Any individual who reports or files a complaint of sexual harassment under this Policy.
Section 3
Prohibited Conduct
The following behaviours are strictly prohibited and constitute violations of this Policy:
  • Unwelcome physical contact of a sexual nature
  • Verbal comments, jokes, or innuendo of a sexual or gender-based nature
  • Displaying, circulating, or sharing sexually suggestive materials
  • Requests for sexual favours in exchange for work-related benefits
  • Persistent unwanted romantic or sexual advances
  • Retaliation against any person who reports or participates in an investigation
Section 4
Reporting Procedures
Any person who experiences or witnesses sexual harassment is encouraged to report it promptly. Reports can be made through the following channels:
  • Direct reporting: To the designated HR Focal Point or Complaint Officer
  • Confidential reporting: Via the anonymous feedback channel [to be designated]
  • External reporting: Through applicable Egyptian labour authorities if internal resolution is unsatisfactory
All reports will be treated with strict confidentiality. The identity of the complainant will not be disclosed without their consent, except where required by law.
